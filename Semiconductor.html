    Conclusion

    We have created a biosensor that is different from the previous one. Advanced from single electrode to double electrode, we chose Au and graphene to make electrodes. The largest difficulty in our ideal "FF" planar double electrode design is the control of the distance between two electrodes. The dense electrode and the electrode should be kept 2μm, which requires precise alignment and long-time process. Althoth the planar electrode is more complicated than the stacked electrode, it has larger surface area and can be connected with more target DNA, which will lead to a more sensitivity biosensor. Although the surface area of the platform electrode is reduced, the measurement method is the same as that of the planar electrode measurement. Therefore, due to the time, we will temporarily adopt the platform electrode as the final test measurement.
