  • Chip Cleaning

    • The surface of the gold electrode needs to be modified to hybrid the DNA. After the chip package is completed, electrodes could be cleaned by cyclic voltammetry. The first wash was carried out at 1 M NaOH at a speed of 0.3 V / s in the range -0.2 to + 0.8 V, followed by a second wash in 0.5 M H2SO4 at a speed of 0.3 V / s and a range of -0.25 to + 1.75 V.

  • The connection between Au and DNA probe

    • There are two curves in each plot. The lines in black show the original state of each chip. And the lines in red show the state which was added 0.1/0.01/0.001 uM DNA probe. As figure, there were significant difference between two curves each plot. Descript that DNA were connected to the electrode successfully.
